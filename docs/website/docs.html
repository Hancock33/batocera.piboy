  • slides (PDF)

Getting support

If you find that you need help with Buildroot, you can ask for help:

  • On the buildroot mailing list at buildroot at buildroot.org
  • On the Buildroot IRC channel, #buildroot on Freenode
  • On our bugtracker.
  • Through the various companies offering commercial Buildroot support.
